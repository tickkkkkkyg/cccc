founder's story

From a student in Nepal to building global infrastructure

Pushkar Budha grew up in Dhangadhi, a city in the far-western plains of Nepal, where he developed a deep curiosity for technology from a young age while studying at Axis Vidhyashram School. Like many students, his journey into the world of servers began with a simple Minecraft server — one he could barely afford to keep running.

Frustrated by the high prices, poor support, and unreliable uptime of existing hosting providers, Pushkar decided to do something about it. He taught himself networking, server management, and infrastructure from the ground up — spending countless nights studying documentation, forums, and YouTube tutorials.

What started as a small project to help friends host their servers cheaply grew into something far bigger. Xyle Hosting was born from that same belief that drove Pushkar on day one: world-class infrastructure shouldn't be a luxury. Today, Xyle serves thousands of clients across the globe — a testament to what passion, grit, and a clear mission can build.

"

I never had access to expensive infrastructure growing up. So I built it — affordable, reliable, and built with the community in mind. Every server we host is a reminder of why we started.

— Pushkar Budha, Founder of Xyle Hosting
