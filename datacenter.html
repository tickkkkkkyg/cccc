Our Data Centers

Enterprise-grade infrastructure hosted in partner facilities — built for speed, uptime, and security.

infrastructure

Hosted in Partner Data Centers

Every Xyle Hosting server runs inside a carefully chosen partner data center. We select facilities based on strict criteria — power redundancy, physical security, cooling, and fiber connectivity — so your servers always have the best possible foundation.

Why Partner Data Centers?

Building and owning data center infrastructure requires enormous capital — and that cost gets passed on to customers. Instead, Xyle Hosting maintains direct partnerships with established, carrier-neutral colocation facilities. This means you get Tier III-equivalent power and cooling, multiple fiber ISPs, 24/7 physical security — all at prices only possible through our partner relationships.
